Features

Exciting reactive turn-based combat.

No random encounters, you can always spot enemies. No grinding, just strategy and smart builds are capable of beating any enemy. Parry attacks, boost your damage through Quick Time Events, put enemies under pressure and stagger them, combine spells and techniques to create powerful reactions.

Freedom to traverse a living world.

Climb, swim, sneak, run, smash and move rocks! A modern movement system where the player can reach almost any place.

Your characters, your build.

Each character begins with a starting job, but you can unlock and switch between more than 25 unique jobs, changing their combat style, attributes, skills, and more.
